Order and delivery process

There are various ways to place an order which you will find under the “Ordering / Payment Options” categories on the Home page. Once payment is confirmed, the ordered items will be drawn from the distribution warehouse and loaded with the software image provided to us by the participating university. The software includes MS-Windows 7 Professional and MS-Office 2010 Professional as predetermined by the universities in accordance with their existing Microsoft Campus License Agreement. Once complete the unit will be repacked in its original packaging and sent for delivery.

 

 

From the date of confirmation of the payment, excluding weekends, orders marked as high priority will be received within 3-4 working days and orders marked as Normal priority will be received within 7 working days. Please ensure that your payment is confirmed as quickly as possible to ensure a speedy process.

 

 

Please note that we do not deliver over weekends.
