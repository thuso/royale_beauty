Payment via cheque

The customer must first logon to validate his or her customer information. Once the validation is successful, will the customer be able to select the required products for purchase. The customer is then required to enter personal information like name, surname and delivery address. The customer must select the “Cheque Deposit” to complete the transaction. This option does not require credit card information. Please keep track of the Order Number being generated by the system for all future reference. Once the “Checkout” is complete, we will be able to track and monitor the order process.
